Comparing two files based on text 
Comand from the terminal: diff -u <new file name> <old file name>
-u stands for uniform
lines deleted are preceeded by -
lines added are preceeded by +
lines unaltered have nothing preceeding them

Version control live examples:
Wikipedia
Dropbox
Google docs

It helps you keep a tab on the chnages you maade and lets you restore to an older version when necessary
